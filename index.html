About The Project

Building the foundation for a smarter, greener campus

Your Voice + Our Digital Campus

This Digital Twin Campus starts with you. Right now, it’s a collaborative platform where students and staff can report sustainability issues (like leaks, waste, or energy waste) directly through this website. Each submission helps us:

  • Pinpoint problem areas on campus with crowd-sourced data
  • Prioritize fixes based on real community needs
  • Build a living map of sustainability challenges

In the future, we aim to integrate real-time sensors and 3D analytics to predict issues before they happen—but today, every complaint you file is a step toward that vision.

"Think of this as Wikipedia for campus sustainability—the more people contribute, the smarter our system becomes."
